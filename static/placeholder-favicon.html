Create Your Favicon

To create a favicon for your website:

  1. Create a 32x32 pixel PNG image with your initials "NW" or a simple design
  2. Save it as "favicon-32x32.png" in the static/ directory
  3. Or use an online favicon generator like favicon.io

For your profile photo:

  1. Add a square photo of yourself (ideally 360x360 pixels or larger)
  2. Save it as "ning-photo.jpg" in the static/ directory
